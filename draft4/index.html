eLux MEDICAL PROCEDURE

During eLux procedure, a solution of gold nanoparticles is injected into fatty tissue.

The nanoparticles diffuse within the target area, evenly distributing throughout the fatty tissue.

800nm laser is used on the tissue to precisely target the treatment area.

Laser activates the nanoparticles and produces local heating that causes fat to be removed from the fat cells.

Liquified fat is removed via the body's natural processes. Optionality of liquid fat to be removed through liposuction.
