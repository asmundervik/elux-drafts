HOW THE ELUX PROCEDURE WORKS

The eLux procedure uses advanced nanotechnology combined with precision laser activation to safely and effectively remove unwanted fat.

Injection

During eLux procedure, a solution of gold nanoparticles is injected into fatty tissue.

Diffusion

The nanoparticles diffuse within the target area, evenly distributing throughout the fatty tissue.

Laser Application

800nm laser is used on the tissue to precisely target the treatment area.

Activation

Laser activates the nanoparticles and produces local heating that causes fat to be removed from the fat cells.

Removal

Liquified fat is removed via the body's natural processes. Optionality of liquid fat to be removed through liposuction.

THE TECHNOLOGY BEHIND ELUX

Gold Nanoparticles

Biocompatible gold nanoparticles are precisely engineered to absorb laser energy and convert it to localized heat.

800nm Laser Precision

The 800nm wavelength is specifically chosen to penetrate tissue effectively while activating the gold nanoparticles.

Selective Targeting

Only fat cells containing nanoparticles are affected, leaving surrounding tissue unharmed.

Minimally Invasive

Simple injection-based delivery system eliminates the need for surgical incisions and extensive tissue manipulation.
